FAQs

1. What should I consider when buying a sofa?

When buying a sofa, it's essential to consider several factors such as size, material, style, and durability. Measure your space beforehand to ensure the sofa will fit comfortably without overcrowding your living room. Consider the material choice, whether leather or fabric, based on your preferences and lifestyle needs. Your style preference should also guide your choice, along with the level of durability required.

2. How can I maintain my sofa to keep it looking good?

Maintaining your sofa involves periodic cleaning with appropriate cleaning agents and brushing off dust regularly using a soft brush or vacuum cleaner attachment. You may also need to fluff up the cushions periodically and plump them up to preserve their shape.

3. Can I get custom-made sofas from these manufacturers?

Yes. Most of these manufacturers offer custom-made sofas tailored to your specific needs and preferences, such as fabric choice, size, style, and shape. Contact the manufacturer to get a quotation and place your order.
