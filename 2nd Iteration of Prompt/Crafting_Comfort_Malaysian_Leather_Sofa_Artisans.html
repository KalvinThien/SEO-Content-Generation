About Us

Dynamic Brands is a furniture manufacturing company based in Malaysia. We specialize in sofa manufacturing and provide our services to furniture buyers, home decor enthusiasts, interior designers, furniture retailers, and homeowners across Malaysia.

Our team of skilled artisans is dedicated to crafting comfortable and stylish leather sofas that meet our clients' unique needs and preferences. We use only the highest quality materials and pay attention to every detail to ensure that each sofa is of the highest standard.

Our Services

Customized leather sofa manufacturing

We provide customized manufacturing of leather sofas that cater to your specific requirements and preferences. Our skilled artisans work with you closely to ensure you get the sofa of your dreams.

Repair and restoration of leather sofas

We provide repair and restoration services for your leather sofas, ensuring that they remain in good condition for a long time. Our team of experts will restore your sofa to its former glory.

Wholesale leather sofa supply to furniture retailers

We provide wholesale supply of leather sofas to furniture retailers, ensuring that they receive high-quality products that meet their customers' needs. Our products are competitively priced, making them an attractive option for retailers.
