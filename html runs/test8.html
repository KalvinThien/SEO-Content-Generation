Frequently Asked Questions

1. How long does it take to manufacture a custom sofa?

The time required to manufacture a custom sofa can vary depending on the complexity of the design, the availability of materials, and the workload of the manufacturer. Generally, it can take anywhere from a few weeks to a couple of months. It's best to discuss the timeline with your chosen manufacturer to get a more accurate estimate.

2. Can I provide my own fabric for the custom sofa?

Yes, many custom sofa manufacturers in Malaysia allow customers to provide their own fabric. However, it's important to ensure that the fabric is suitable for upholstery and meets the manufacturer's requirements in terms of quality and durability.

3. How much does a custom sofa from Malaysia cost?

The cost of a custom sofa can vary depending on factors such as the design complexity, size, chosen materials, and additional features. It's recommended to discuss your budget and requirements with the manufacturer to get an accurate price quote.

4. Can I make changes to the design after placing an order?

Most custom sofa manufacturers allow for design modifications within a certain timeframe after placing the order. However, keep in mind that major changes might incur additional costs or delay the production process. It's best to discuss any desired design changes as early as possible to avoid complications.

5. Do custom sofa manufacturers provide delivery services?

Yes, most custom sofa manufacturers offer delivery services to ensure that your newly crafted sofa reaches your doorstep safely. Delivery options and charges may vary, so it's advisable to inquire about this during the ordering process.
