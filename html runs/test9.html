Frequently Asked Questions

1. How long does it take to manufacture a custom sofa?

The manufacturing time for a custom sofa can vary depending on factors such as the complexity of the design, the availability of materials, and the workload of the manufacturer. On average, it takes several weeks to complete the manufacturing process. It's recommended to consult with the manufacturer for a more accurate estimate based on your specific requirements.

2. Can I choose the upholstery fabric for my custom sofa?

Absolutely! With custom sofa manufacturing services in Malaysia, you have the freedom to choose from a wide range of upholstery fabrics, including luxurious fabrics, durable leather, or even eco-friendly options. The manufacturers will guide you through the selection process, ensuring that the chosen fabric complements your design and meets your desired level of comfort and quality.

3. Are custom sofas more expensive than ready -made options?

Custom sofas are often perceived as a higher-end option, but the price can vary depending on various factors such as the design complexity, materials used, and additional features. While custom sofas may have a higher price tag compared to ready-made options, they offer unparalleled craftsmanship, customization, and the opportunity to own a truly unique piece of furniture.

4. Can I make changes to the design during the manufacturing process?

Most custom sofa manufacturers in Malaysia are flexible and allow design modifications during the manufacturing process, within a certain timeframe. However, major changes might affect the timeline and incur additional costs. It's best to communicate your design preferences clearly at the beginning and discuss any desired changes with the manufacturer as early as possible.

5. Can I expect delivery and installation services for my custom sofa?

Yes, custom sofa manufacturers in Malaysia typically offer delivery and installation services to ensure a seamless experience. They take utmost care in handling and delivering your sofa, ensuring it arrives at your doorstep in pristine condition. The installation process is also carried out professionally to guarantee that your sofa is set up correctly and ready to enhance your living space.
