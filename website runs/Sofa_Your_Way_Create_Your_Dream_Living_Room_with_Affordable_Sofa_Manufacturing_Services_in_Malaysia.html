We are a trusted and reliable sofa manufacturing company based in Malaysia. Our aim is to help customers create their dream living room at an affordable price without compromising on quality. With years of experience in the industry, our team of skilled craftsmen is committed to creating bespoke sofas that cater to your specific needs and preferences. Whether you're looking for a classic design or a modern piece, we have got you covered.

Services

Custom-Made Sofas

If you're looking for a one-of-a-kind sofa, our custom-made service is perfect for you. We work closely with you to understand your vision and create a sofa that fits your style, budget, and space.

Sofa Repair and Restoration

Is your beloved sofa showing signs of wear and tear? Our repair and restoration service can help give it a new lease of life. Our team of experts will fix any damage, reupholster if necessary, and restore it to its former glory.

FAQs

What materials do you use?

We use high-quality materials sourced from reliable suppliers to ensure durability and comfort. You can choose from a wide range of fabrics, leathers, and finishes to suit your preferences.

How long does it take to make a custom-made sofa?

The time taken to make a custom-made sofa depends on the complexity of the design and size. Usually, it takes about 4 to 6 weeks from design approval to delivery.

Do you offer a warranty?

Yes, we offer a standard one-year warranty for all our sofas against manufacturing defects.
