Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to create bespoke seating solutions?

The length of the creation process depends on the complexity of the seating solution. Generally, the more complicated the design, the longer it will take to create. However, we are committed to providing our clients with high-quality furniture within a reasonable time frame and will work with you throughout the entire process.

What kind of materials do you use for your furniture?

Sit in Style only uses high-quality materials for our furniture to ensure its durability and longevity. We use a variety of materials including leather, fabric, and wood.

Do you offer delivery and installation services?

Yes, we provide full delivery and installation services for all of our seating solutions. Our team will work with you to ensure that your furniture is installed properly and in a timely manner.

What is your return policy?

We want all of our clients to be completely satisfied with their furniture. If you have any issues or concerns with your order, please contact us directly and we will do our best to resolve the issue. We do not offer returns on customized furniture.
