What is the average cost of an office chair in Malaysia?
The cost of an office chair in Malaysia can vary depending on the make, quality, and other features. Generally, however, good quality budget chairs can cost as little as RM50-RM100 while moderately-priced chairs are around RM200-RM400 with high-end office chairs going for between RM500 to over RM2000
What should you look for in an office chair?
When choosing an office chair, it is important to consider comfort, adjustability and ergonomics. Look for a chair that has a comfortable seat and backrest, height adjustment abilities, lumbar support, and armrests.
