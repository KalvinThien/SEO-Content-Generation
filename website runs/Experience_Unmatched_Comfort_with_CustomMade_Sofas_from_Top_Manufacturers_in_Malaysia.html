FAQs

What are custom-made sofas?

Custom-made sofas refer to furniture pieces that are made to order according to the client's specifications and requirements. These sofas are unique and personalized, offering an unbeatable level of comfort and tailored design.

Why choose a custom-made sofa over a ready-made one?

Choosing a custom-made sofa allows you to have complete control over its design, style, materials, and dimensions, ensuring that it meets your specific preferences. This way, you can get a piece of furniture that suits your needs in terms of space, comfort, and aesthetic appeal.

How long does it take to make a custom-made sofa in Malaysia?

The timeline for making a custom-made sofa depends on the complexity of the design and the manufacturer's workload. Generally, it takes about three weeks to two months to produce a custom-made sofa in Malaysia.

How much does a custom-made sofa cost?

The cost of a custom-made sofa varies depending on several factors such as size, material choice, level of customization, and the complexity of the design. Typically, expect to pay anywhere from RM1000 upwards for a basic custom-made sofa in Malaysia.
