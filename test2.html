Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. How long does it take for a custom-made sofa to be delivered?

The delivery time depends on the complexity of the design and the materials used, but usually, it takes between 4-8 weeks for a custom-made sofa to be delivered.

2. Are eco-friendly sofas more expensive than traditional sofas?

Not necessarily. Although some eco-friendly materials such as organic cotton or recycled plastic might be slightly more expensive than traditional materials, sustainable sofa manufacturers are often able to offer competitive prices due to lower production costs.

3. What is the Warranty period for sofas manufactured in Malaysia?

The warranty period for sofas in Malaysia varies depending on the manufacturer and the type of sofa. Generally, it ranges from 1-5 years for structural damage and defects.
